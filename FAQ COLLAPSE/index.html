Frequently Asked Questions (F.A.Q.)

When will my order be delivered?

You can reach the information on which day your order will be delivered via the sms sent to you when it is received.

How can I find out the status of my order?

You can find out by inquiring with the delivery number on our website or you can contact us by calling our call center at 0850 999 99 99.

Can someone else pick up my order?

Yes, delivery can be made to a different person at your address, in exchange for a ID number and signature.

Where can I find the order tracking number?

The delivery number of your order is also your shipment tracking number. You can access this information in the sms content sent to you or the shipment tracking link in your membership.

My order has not arrived, what should I do?

In case of a first call to your address, the next business day (including Saturday) will be called again.
